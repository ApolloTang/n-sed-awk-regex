The 12 metacharacters characters:

    the backslash \
    the caret ^
    the dollar sign $
    the period or dot .
    the vertical bar or pipe symbol |
    the question mark ?
    the asterisk or star *
    the plus sign +
    the opening parenthesis (
    the closing parenthesis )
    the opening square bracket [
    the opening curly brace {

lose their special meaning when preceed by a backspace.

Backspace when used before a non-metacharacter, can produce
different meanings depending upon the version of the program
For example some versions treat \<, \>, \1, etc
as metasequences.
