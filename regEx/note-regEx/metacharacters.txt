There are 12 characters with special meanings:


 1   \  backslash
 2   ^  caret
 3   $  dollar sign
 4   .  period or dot
 5   |  vertical bar or pipe symbol
 6   ?  question mark
 7   *  asterisk or star
 8   +  plus sign
 9   -  minus sign
10   () parenthesis
11   [] square bracket
12   {} curly braces

These special characters are often called "metacharacters"
