Consider the following: /^From|Subject|Date: ./ /^(From|Subject|Date): ./ Both appear to matches lines beginning with "From: .", "Subject .", or "Date: .". But the first one won't work -- the regEx is composed of three alternatives that match: "^From" or "Subject" or "Date: ." which is not particularly useful. If you want the leading caret and trailing dot apply to each alternative, use parentheses to "constrain" the alternation.
